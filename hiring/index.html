Open Positions

(Last edit: June 2026)

  • Postdoctoral researchers — Contact us with your CV and a cover letter describing 1) how you can contribute to the group and 2) your specific research interests. Postdocs bringing new materials insight and complementary application experience, such as optical system design or device fabrication skills, are particularly welcome.
  • Graduate students, undergraduates, and visiting scholars — Inquiries are welcome at any time; please reach out with your CV.


For 2026-2027, we are particularly looking to grow in the following areas:

  • Molecular synthesis, structure modification and smart synthesis of low-dimensional organic-inorganic hybrid metal-halide perovskites.
  • Optical system design and fabrication for miniaturized chiroptics and chiral photonics.
  • Non-linear chiroptics.
  • Mathematical and mechanical modeling of synthetic solid-state materials.


You can reach us directly at swh@uga.edu.
